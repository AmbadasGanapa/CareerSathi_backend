A.GCareerSathi FAQ

1) Who is this for?
A.GCareerSathi is for students in Class 10, 12, and Diploma tracks who want career direction support in India.

2) How long does the assessment take?
Usually around 10-12 minutes.

3) What is the refund policy?
Users can refer to the Refund Policy page for complete terms. Refunds are handled as per published policy and payment-verification status.

4) What do I get in the report?
Top recommended branches, likely-fit courses, action steps, and scholarship/exam guidance.

5) Can I retake the test?
Yes. You can attempt again and track history from your dashboard.
