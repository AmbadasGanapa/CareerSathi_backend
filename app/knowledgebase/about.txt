About AGCareerSathi


AGCareerSathi is plantform developed for students studied 10th,12th,diploma to choose their further career options
Developed by Ambadas Ganapa

Along with this recommendation it also offers several services such as 1:1 mentoring , Resume/CV Review, Career Workshop,Build Your Online Identity,Academic Study Planning,Future Skills Hub etc 
Our mission is to empower students with personalized career guidance, helping them make informed decisions about their future paths. We aim to bridge the gap between education and career opportunities, ensuring that every student has access to the resources they need to succeed in their chosen fields.
